St. Benilde Romancon

Sanctification by doing "ordinary things in extraordinary ways" - Pope Paul VI

Patron Saint of Vocations

  • Born on Thuret, France on June 14, 1805
  • Baptized as Pierre Romancon
  • Died August 13, 1862
  • Beatified by Pius XII on April 4, 1948
  • Canonized by Paul VI on October 29, 1967
  • Six schools in the Philippines are named after him.
